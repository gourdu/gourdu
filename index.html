Go Urdu

WatchOS iOS tvOS Android

Spoken by over 160 million people, UK’s 4th main language. Why haven’t you learnt Urdu yet?

FEATURES

WatchOS

Start, escalate and end an Urdu conversation in real-time, using commonly used Urdu phrases, available at the flick of the wrist.

iOS/Android

Learn background knowledge about the Urdu language, to assist with speaking and understanding Urdu natively.

tvOS

Watch real-life Urdu conversations to learn key Urdu words and phrases, from the comfort of your TV.
